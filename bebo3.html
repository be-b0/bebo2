BORG EL ARAB

Borg El Arab Beach Resort has a garden, terrace, a restaurant and bar in Dawwār ‘Abd al Qādir Qāsim. This 5-star hotel offers room service. The hotel also features free WiFi and free private parking. Guests at the hotel can enjoy a buffet breakfast. Speaking Arabic, German, English and French at the reception, staff are ready to help around the clock. The nearest airport is Borg el Arab International Airport, 34 km from Borg El |Arab Beach Resort.
